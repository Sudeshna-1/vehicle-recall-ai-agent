About Recalls

What is a Vehicle Recall?

When a safety defect is identified, manufacturers and the DVSA issue a recall to protect road users.

Safety Defect

A recall is raised when a manufacturer or DVSA identifies a fault that may endanger drivers, passengers, or other road users.

Free of Charge

All recall repairs are completed free at an authorised dealer, regardless of age, mileage, or warranty status.

Stay Informed

Manufacturers must contact registered owners by law. Use this tool to independently verify — even if you've received no letter.
